Pokimane And JiDion Are Collaborating After A Heated Controversy Between Them

June 09, 2024
blog

Once upon a time, a YouTuber stole a picture of a Twitch broadcaster sans makeup and turned it into a meme. The joke’s on him since she immediately tweeted the identical picture with a comment that’s much too serious to laugh at. Is this the end, or a fresh beginning?

If you couldn’t tell, this narrative was inspired by the JiDion-Pokimane spat that dominated headlines earlier this year. With the two creators teaming together for a collaboration, the feud is officially gone.

Pokimane Adopted a Makeup-Free Look

Pokimane tweeted a selfie of herself without makeup as soon as JiDion released the collaborative Q&A video on his YouTube channel — the same one that netizens, including JiDion, had used to insult her in January. However, it was the comment that accompanied the picture that drew everyone’s attention. It said:

“This face earns more money than you’ll ever see in your whole life.”

Of course, just below that tweet, the Twitch broadcaster came clean with her followers and revealed that the post was a dare from JiDion, which you already know if you’ve seen his YouTube video, which has over 4.2 million views.

JiDion urged Pokimane to embrace his sense of humor.

JiDion posted a video of the two coming together for a Chick-Fil-A mukbang on his side of the video streaming globe, YouTube. He stated that he was very inebriated when he used Pokimane’s famed no makeup photo as his Twitter profile image after being banned from Twitch.

Still, on Twitter, he advised her to embrace the snapshot with open arms and lean into the comedy a little more. He sent her a picture with the comment, “This face earns more money than you will ever make.” Pokimane obliged and didn’t even modify a word once the steak was gone!
