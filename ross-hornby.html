Soon, Alex Smith was roped in and the venture was renamed.

Gradually, the trio managed to grab attention in the online gaming scenario. Moreover, their filming and editing skills were unique and innovative. Besides making gaming videos, ‘HAT Films’ began creating gaming trailers. They were approached by ‘Studiosos’ to creatMinecraftft’ skin trailers for their Xbox version. ‘HAT Films’ has its very own YouTube channel and provides gameplays of popular video games such as ‘Grand Theft Auto 5,’ ‘Trials,’ ‘Worms,’ and ‘TTT.’ They also create comedy sketches and skits.
