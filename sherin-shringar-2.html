Wiki/Biography

Sherin Shringar was born on 5 May 1987 (age 32 years; as in 2019) in Bengaluru. Her zodiac sign is Taurus.

Sherin As A Child

She did her schooling from Kaveri School in Bengaluru. She went to Baldwin’s Women’s Methodist College but discontinued her schooling to pursue her modelling career.

Career

She started her career as a model in her teens. She made her debut as a child artist in the 2002 Kannada film “Police Dog.”

She then appeared in the Kannada film “Dhruva (2002).”

She rose to fame after she made her Tamil film debut with the “Thulluvadho Ilamai (2002).”

She then made her debut in Telugu films with the movie “Juniors (2003),” the remake of  “Thulluvadho Ilamai.”
