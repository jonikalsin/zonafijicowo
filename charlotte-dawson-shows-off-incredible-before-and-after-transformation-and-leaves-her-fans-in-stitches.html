Charlotte Dawson shows off incredible before and after transformation and leaves her fans in stitche

June 26, 2024
blog

CHARLOTTE Dawson has taken to Instagram to show off her incredible before and after transformation – leaving her fans in stitches.

The mother-of-one showed off how fake tan and make-up altered her appearance in a side-by-side comparison as she told her following she was “feeling fabulous”.

Charlotte, 29, posed in a pink hat, brown boots and a patterned jumpsuit for the transformation pictures before and after getting glammed up.

In one of the snaps, the Ex On The Beach star – who is the daughter of comedian Les Dawson – could be seen pulling a face and holding up a peace sign.

Opting for an au-natural look, the reality TV favourite revealed her face without make-up and her fake-tan free skin as she joked she was a “pale thumb”.

Whereas the second snapped saw her golden-brown, wearing lipstick and false eye-lashes and with perfectly blow-dried brunette tresses.

“Here she chuffin is,” Charlotte captioned the photos. “Pale thumb to tanned thumbalina. No better feeling than feeling fabulous.”

Fans were left in hysterics over the two photographs as they could not believe the transformation between Charlotte’s before and after.

“Stop it!” wrote celebrity pal Kate Lawler, as a fan chimed in: “You crack me up!”

A third person added: “Uncanny,” while others commented laughing emojis.

It comes days after Charlotte risked revealing all as she posed knickerless on the red carpet at ITV Palooza – risking baring all.

The reality TV star – who gave birth to her son Noah in January – showed off her endless legs in the incredible floral dress.

She stunned in the sheer dress that exposed her legs and hip thanks to a split that reached her waist.

Comedian Les Dawson’s daughter wore her hair in Hollywood waves, and teamed her dress with sheer stilettos and a diamante necklace.

She also shared the carpet with her mum Tracy, who raised her alone after Les sadly passed away when Charlotte was just a baby.

Among other celebrities pictured at the Royal Festival Hall on Southbank, in London, last night were partners in crime Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield.

The pair appeared alongside a host of famous faces from the channel’s biggest shows including Susanna Reid, Lorraine Kelly, and Dermot O’Leary.
