Thomas Rhetts Life Changes Tour is Coming to Canada in 2019 Celeb Secrets Country

September 09, 2024
blog

Thomas Rhett is headed to Canada!

The multi-platinum singer/songwriter will bring his wildly popular Life Changes Tour up North in 2019 with rising crooner Dustin Lynch beginning on April 24th in Montreal, QC. The run follows a string-of sell outs in the U.S. as fans flock to see Rhett’s fiery hot live show.

“My fans in Canada are incredible, so I’m really excited that we will be headed there for a couple weeks next year and get to bring all the energy of the Life Changes tour with us,” Rhett said.

Tickets for the Canadian tour run go on sale this Friday, September 28 at 10:00A local time via ThomasRhett.com.

Check out the list of dates below and let us know if you’ll be catching a show by sending us a tweet at @CS_Country.
