What’s going on? Olajuwon Dickerson and Katina Goode, have they reconciled?

Katina Goode and Olajuwon Dickerson headed on a romantic vacation one month after announcing their split

Married at First Sight stars Katina Goode and Olajuwon Dickerson announced their divorce last month, but their recent tropical getaway emphasizes or else.

Fans are stunned after the exes shared photos from their recent trip to Mexico. The 29-years old reality tv star took to Instagram on Friday, December 9, to share a photo of herself with Dickerson, who were both smiling and appeared to be in a positive state.

Katina is seen in the photo resting her leg atop her estranged husband’s while relaxing at the Hilton Tulum Riviera Maya Resort. She also captioned the post that reads,

“If we take off now we can catch the sun,”

Olajuwon, on the other hand, posted more photos from their trip, including pictures of them on a boat and cuddling on an airplane. Also, he gushes over his estranged wife in the caption section, writing,

“Never forget your [sic] a fearless women! Thank you for always having faith in whatever my heart and mind desires in life. Thank you for making sure I have a strong relationship with god,”

“I appreciate everything you have taught me about myself without you even knowing it.”

Adding to that, he went on to praise Married at First Sight production, penning sown,

“I also want to thank MAFS that I met @__slimmgoodie no matter what life brings to us! God is good in so many ways I get emotional just thinking of my life and how much I have matured as a man. I am truly thankful words can’t express it.”

Is this an official confirmation of their reconciliation? Well, the two have not yet addressed their relationship status, including if this is just a friendly reunion or a romantic one.

How long have Katina Goode and Olajuwon Dickerson been together?

Goode and Dickerson met during Married at First Sight season 14 and married in July 2021 after meeting at the altar. The couple, however, called it quits last month.

The two announced their separation in early November with a joint statement on Instagram. The statement reads,

“After one year of marriage and careful thought and consideration, we have decided to part ways. However, this was not an easy decision to make,”

“We leave our marriage with enormous love and respect for each other. We only ask that, at this time, our privacy is respected.”
