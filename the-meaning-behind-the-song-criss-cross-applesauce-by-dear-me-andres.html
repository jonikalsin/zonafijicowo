The Lyrics

“Criss Cross Applesauce” delves deep into the complexities of human relationships, intimacy, and the yearning for love. The song’s lyrics paint a vivid picture of the inner conflict experienced by the two protagonists. In the first verse, Luke Kanipe sings about the fear of emotional connection, choosing to avoid eye contact to escape the vulnerability it brings. Despite their efforts to convince themselves otherwise, they crave love and intimacy, finding solace in fleeting encounters.

The chorus, beautifully performed by Andrés, speaks of sacrificing one’s own well-being for the sake of rendezvous in the bedroom. The character expresses the toll it takes on their grades and friendships, highlighting the struggle between desire and responsibility. The repetitive use of “ooh ma” adds a touch of vulnerability, unraveling the layers of emotional turmoil experienced.

In the second verse, Kanipe imagines an alternate reality where he finds peace in solitude, gazing at the sea. However, in their current reality, he is simply a wave crashing at the feet of his beloved. His love language becomes her name, echoing the desire to be heard and acknowledged.

Andrés takes the spotlight in the third verse, recounting a conversation with his partner. Amidst their lunch break in the library, he declares his aspirations of becoming a rockstar. However, his partner suggests the more practical path of becoming a doctor to gain her parents’ approval. The clash between personal dreams and societal expectations reflects the ongoing battle between love and individual identity.

The chorus resurfaces, intensifying the emotional weight of the song. The lyrics speak to the consequences of pursuing passion at the expense of other aspects of life. Grades suffer, friendships falter, and the individual’s sense of self begins to erode.

The Outro

The song concludes with an outro by Kanipe, where he admits his addiction to the euphoria and adrenaline that love brings. He feels invincible, soaring beyond limits, even though the world around him may crumble.

The Meaning

“Criss Cross Applesauce” is a profoundly introspective song that dives deep into the complexities of human emotions and relationships. It explores the tug-of-war between the desire for intimate connections and the fear of vulnerability and its potential consequences. The lyrics encapsulate the internal battles faced by individuals in their pursuit of love and self-fulfillment, ultimately leaving them questioning their own choices and sacrificing their own well-being.

This powerful collaboration between Dear Me & Andrés serves as a poignant reminder of the intricacies of human connection and the intricate balance between love, individuality, and societal expectations. It encourages listeners to confront their own struggles and embrace the vulnerability that comes with genuine emotional connections.

In conclusion, “Criss Cross Applesauce” by Dear Me & Andrés resonates deeply with anyone who has experienced the tumultuous journey of love and self-discovery. Its evocative lyrics and captivating melody serve as a testament to the universal experience of the human heart. So sit back, immerse yourself in the music, and let it guide you through the labyrinth of emotions that define us all.
