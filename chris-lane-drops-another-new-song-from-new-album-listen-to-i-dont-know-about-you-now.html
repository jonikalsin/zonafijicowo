Chris Lane just dropped ANOTHER song from his upcoming album Laps Around The Sun and we’re SO here for it!

Titled “I Don’t Know About You” and written by Ashley Gorley, Michael Hardy, Hunter Phelps and Jameson Rodgers, the irresistible track captures the can’t-get-enough feeling of a new relationship. With a clever turn of phrase, the song paints a picture with Country lyrics sure to inspire stadium-wide sing-alongs.

“I Don’t Know About You” joins previously released summer jam “Fishin,'” “Drunk People,” and “Take Back Home Girl” with Tori Kelly on Laps Around the Sun, which drops on Friday, July 13th.
