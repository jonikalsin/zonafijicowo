Neha Bhasin is a famous Indian singer and songwriter. Neha Bhasin is best known for her songs in Bollywood, Tollywood, and her independent songs. Currently, she is participating in Bigg Boss OTT as a contestant.

Early Life

Neha Bhasin was born on November 18, 1982, and is currently 40 years old. Born in New Delhi, India, Neha is Indian by nationality. Likewise, she is from a Hindu Punjabi family. Being born on November 18, her birth sign is Scorpio.

Talking about her family, Neha was born to her father Ashok Bhasin, and her mother Rekha Bhasin. Similarly, she has two siblings; an elder sister named Rashi Bhasin and a younger brother named Anubhav Bhasin.

Education

Moving toward her educational background, Neha completed her schooling at Frank Anthony Public School. During her school days, she took part in her first-ever singing competition award and won it.

Neha was just nine years old then. She always dreamed of becoming a pop star in her childhood days.

Moreover, she joined Lady Sri Ram College for her higher studies and graduated from there. In her early days, she took dance classes at Shiamak Davar’s dance academy. In addition, she also attended vocal classes with Ustad Ghulam Mustafa Khan.

Relationship Status

Neha Bhasin is in a married relationship with Sameer Uddin. Sameer is a popular Indian music composer. The couple dated for a few years and got married at a Western wedding in Tuscany, Italy on October 23, 2016.

The beautiful couple hasn’t become parents till now and is enjoying their married life.

Professional Life

While Neha Bhasin was attending her higher education at Lady Sri Ram College in 2002, she gave an audition for Coke [V] Popstars. Coke [V] Popstars is a national talent hunt managed by Channel V to be a part of the 5-girl pop group.

Neha won the competitions with other four girls and formed India’s first all-girls Viva (band). However, the band members got separated and the band broke up in 2004.

After the break up of the band, Neha began singing for Bollywood films and the Tamil music industry. Her first song in Bollywood was “Kuch Khaas hai” released in 2007. The song gave her a breakthrough and also her first Filmfare nomination in 2008.

Likewise, her first Tamil song is “Pesugiren Pesugiren”. Apart from that, she also participated in the Colors TV reality show Jhalak in 2012. Currently, she is participating in Bigg Boss OTT as a contestant.

Achievements

In 2007, Neha won the Vijay TV Awards under the category “Best Playback Singer”. Likewise, in 2011, she won the Stardust Awards under the category “Musical Sensation of the Year”.

Some of her other awards are the South Indian International Movie Awards under the category “Best Female Playback Singer (Telugu)” (2014), Stardust Awards under the category “Best Playback Singer” (2016), Filmfare Awards (2017, 2018), ZeeCine Awards (2017), and more.
